ABOUT

Sir Timothy John Berners-Lee OM KBE FRS FREng FRSA FBCS, also known as TimBL, is an English computer scientist best known as the inventor of the World Wide Web.

He is also Director of the World Wide Web Consortium, a global web standards organisation he founded in 1994 to lead the web to its full potential. Sir Tim has advised a number of governments and corporations on ongoing digital strategies. A graduate of Oxford University.
